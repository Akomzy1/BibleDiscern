## Description (4000 chars max)

When you face a crossroads — a career change, a relationship decision, a call to something new — where do you turn?

BibleDiscern is an AI-powered Christian Discernment Companion that walks you through a structured, Scripture-rooted journey to seek God's wisdom on life's hardest decisions. From the Latin *librato* — to weigh, to balance, to ponder. Weigh it with wisdom.

**THE 7-STEP DISCERNMENT JOURNEY**

BibleDiscern doesn't just surface Bible verses — it walks alongside you through a complete, theologically grounded process:

1. **The Crossroads** — Name and frame your decision clearly
2. **The Word** — Receive curated Scripture and a Biblical Narrative Match (which figure in Scripture faced something similar to your situation?)
3. **The Tradition** — Explore how the Church has historically approached decisions like yours
4. **The Reason** — Work through the practical dimensions: pros, cons, what each path requires
5. **The Community** — Be reminded of the voices in your life who should speak into this decision
6. **The Stillness** — A 90-second guided contemplative pause (1 Kings 19:12 — "a still, small voice")
7. **The Prayer** — Receive a fully personalized prayer written around your specific situation

**BIBLICAL NARRATIVE MATCHING**

When you bring your situation to BibleDiscern, the AI identifies which biblical figure walked a similar path — Moses choosing to leave the palace, Gideon being called despite his fear, Esther standing at her own crossroads. You're invited into that story, not just handed advice.

**THE FRUIT OF THE SPIRIT DIAGNOSTIC**

Premium users receive a Fruit of the Spirit lens on their decision — love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control — with Scripture references for each fruit applied to their specific situation. A spiritually rich way to evaluate which path produces the fruit you're cultivating.

**THE SPIRITUAL JOURNAL**

Every discernment session is saved as an Ebenezer stone — "Thus far the Lord has helped us" (1 Samuel 7:12). Return to your journal to see how past decisions unfolded. Premium users receive follow-up reminders at 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months to record what happened.

**THE DAILY MOMENT**

Each morning, BibleDiscern sends a brief Scripture reflection — a quiet anchor to start the day in the Word before the day's demands begin.

**PRIVACY FIRST**

Your prayers, decisions, and journal entries are yours alone. We will never sell your spiritual data. We will never use your discernment sessions to train AI models. Everything is encrypted in transit and at rest, accessible only to you.

**WHAT BIBLEDISCERN IS NOT**

BibleDiscern is a spiritual reflection tool. It does not provide pastoral counseling, psychological advice, medical guidance, or financial planning. It does not replace the voice of your pastor, spiritual director, or community. For crisis situations, the app provides immediate access to the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline and other resources.

## App Review Notes

BibleDiscern is a Christian spiritual reflection tool that guides users through a structured, Scripture-based discernment process for personal decision-making.

**AI Content:** The AI component (Anthropic Claude API) provides contextual Bible verses, biblical narrative connections, theological tradition references, and personalized prayers. All AI-generated content includes a disclaimer: "This is a spiritual reflection tool. It does not constitute pastoral counseling or professional advice of any kind. Always weigh AI guidance against Scripture, your own conscience, and the counsel of trusted human advisors."

**No False Claims:** BibleDiscern does not claim to speak for God, deliver prophecy, or guarantee outcomes. The app explicitly positions itself as a *reflection* tool, not a divine oracle.

**Crisis Safety:** The app includes crisis detection and a dedicated CrisisScreen that prominently displays the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline (call or text, free, 24/7), Crisis Text Line, and other resources before any app content is shown.

**In-App Purchases:** Monthly ($7.99) and annual ($49.99) Premium subscriptions via StoreKit. A 7-day free trial is included. All purchases are processed through Apple's standard IAP mechanism via RevenueCat.

**Sign In with Apple:** Implemented using expo-apple-authentication. Required for apps offering third-party social login.
